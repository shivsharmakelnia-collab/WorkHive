Your Journey with WORKHIVE

Follow these simple steps to begin your professional trading journey.

01. Purchase a Plan

Click the Buy Now button, create your WORKHIVE account, choose your preferred plan, and complete your payment securely.

02. Registration Confirmation

After your payment is successfully verified, a member of our support team will contact you and explain the complete onboarding process.

03. Professional Training

Receive structured training to understand trading fundamentals, platform usage, company workflow, and risk management before you begin.

04. Trading Account

Every participant should have access to a supported funded trading account. If you don't already have one, our customer support team can guide you through obtaining one, including available partner offers if applicable.

05. Daily Guidance

Our team provides daily guidance according to the company's workflow. Trading always involves risk, so follow the training and company policies carefully.

06. Important Policy

If a participant trades independently and violates account rules or causes an account breach, responsibility remains with the participant. If an issue occurs because of a verified company-side operational mistake, the company will review the case according to its official policy.
